ML Architecture

Built on intelligence,
not guesswork.

Custora AI is built on a calibrated Logistic Regression pipeline optimized for churn recall — ensuring high-risk customers are identified early with interpretable probability scores.

Random Forest Classifier

Aggregates hundreds of decision trees to capture complex, nonlinear behavioral signals. Robust against overfitting with high interpretability across customer segments.

Primary Model

XGBoost Gradient Boosting

Sequential ensemble learning that corrects prediction errors iteratively. Excels at capturing subtle interaction effects between usage frequency and contract variables.

Ensemble Layer

Logistic Regression

Calibrated probability estimator providing interpretable risk scores. Serves as a transparent baseline for explainability reports delivered to customer success teams.

Baseline + Calibration

SHAP Explainability

SHapley Additive exPlanations surfaces the exact features driving each prediction — tenure, support tickets, plan tier — giving CS teams actionable, human-readable reasoning behind every churn score.

Interpretability Layer

Weighted Voting Ensemble

All three models vote on each customer's churn probability, weighted by individual holdout validation performance. The result: a final churn score that outperforms any single model — with quantified confidence intervals surfaced directly in the PDF report.
